Springhill Academy History:

springhill academy was founded in 1998 and it started as a daycare.After three years, nursery was established and laiter that same year which was the year 2000 the junior primary was also established. At the end of 2001 the school had already grown and it had about 1150 pupils and 60 teachers.In springhill academy we offer British learning system and studenta are also able to develop their talent by participate in curiculum activities like all type of sports, drama, talent show and music.
